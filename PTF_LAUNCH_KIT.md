## Launch Checklist

- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_X_URL=https://x.com/PTF_sol`.
- Confirm `NEXT_PUBLIC_CA` and `SOURCE_TOKEN_MINT` are the live $PTF mint.
- Confirm `NEXT_PUBLIC_BUY_URL` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_PUMPFUN_URL` point to the $PTF Pump.fun page.
- Confirm `NEXT_PUBLIC_DEXSCREENER_URL` points to the live $PTF DexScreener pair.
- Confirm `ELIGIBILITY_MIN=1000000`.
- Confirm `EPOCH_MINUTES=5` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_EPOCH_MINUTES=5`.
- Confirm `CLAIM_ENABLED`, `BUY_ENABLED`, and `AIRDROP_ENABLED` only go true when treasury is funded and ready.
- Confirm `TREASURY_WALLET_SECRET` is set only in Railway, never Vercel public envs.
- Confirm site shows PTF, Pump Treasury Fund, $PTF, and no old branding.

## Risk Line

PTF is an experimental Pump.fun treasury protocol. Digital assets are highly volatile and may lose some or all of their value. Verify all addresses, eligibility rules and onchain activity independently.
